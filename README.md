## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive tactical analysis of **AFC Bournemouth's 2024/25 Premier League season**, using event-level football data to evaluate the team's attacking patterns, defensive structure, passing networks, and individual player performances.

The objective is to uncover the tactical principles behind Andoni Iraola's playing style through advanced football analytics and data visualization.

The analysis combines statistical metrics with visual storytelling to provide actionable insights into Bournemouth's performance throughout the season.

# 📊 Key Findings

### High Pressing Team

Bournemouth ranked among the Premier League's most aggressive pressing teams, maintaining an average defensive height of approximately **43–44 meters** and a **PPDA of around 10**, reflecting Iraola's high-intensity pressing philosophy.

---

### Attacking Style

- Heavy reliance on wide attacks.
- Right flank generated over **56%** of final-third entries.
- Limited central penetration.
- Quick transitions after regaining possession.

---

### Passing Style

- Average passing height of **50.45 meters**.
- High defensive line.
- Strong vertical progression.
- One of the league's highest long-pass percentages.

---

### Defensive Shape

The defensive block remains compact and narrow, prioritizing central protection while encouraging opponents toward wider areas.
